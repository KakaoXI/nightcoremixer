# 🎧 Nightcore Mixer

![Nightcore Mixer Preview](https://files.catbox.moe/2o74s0.png)

**Nightcore Mixer** is a modern web application that allows you to create nightcore-style audio mixes directly in your browser — no software installation required.

Upload your favorite music and transform it in real time with powerful audio processing tools designed for both casual users and advanced audio enthusiasts.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎵 Upload Your Music
- Supports **.mp3**, **.wav**, and **.flac** audio files  
- Drag & drop support  
- Load **multiple files or entire folders** to create your own custom playlist  

---

### ⚡ Real-Time Audio Processing
Modify your track live while listening:

- 🔊 Volume control  
- ⏩ Speed up / Slow down playback  
- 🎶 Pitch adjustment (Nightcore / Slowed styles)  
- 🌫️ Reverb effects  
- 🔈 Bass Boost enhancement  

All effects are applied instantly in your browser.

---

### 🧠 Advanced Controls
Nightcore Mixer also includes additional advanced settings for experienced users:

- Fine-tune playback parameters  
- Adjust audio characteristics beyond basic nightcore effects  
- Customize your sound profile in real time  

---

### 📤 Export Your Mix
When you're satisfied with your mix, you can:

- Export your edited audio directly as a **.wav** file  
- Download your custom nightcore version instantly
